Proposition.
[2.6] Let $a,b$ be integers, not both zero, and let $d$ be the positive integer which generates the subgroup $a\mathbb{{Z}}+b\mathbb{{Z}}$. Then
  1. $d$ can be written in the form $d=ar + bs$ for some integers $r$ and $s$ .
  2. $d$ divides $a$ and $b$ .
  3. If an integer $e$ divides $a$ and $b$, it also divides $d$ .
