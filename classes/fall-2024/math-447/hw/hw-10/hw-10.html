Problem.

  1. Show $$ U(f^2, P) - L(f^2, P) \leq 2B[U(f, P) - L(f, P)] $$ for all partitions $ P $ of $[a, b]$. Hint: $ f(x)^2 - f(y)^2 = [f(x) + f(y)] \cdot [f(x) - f(y)] $.
