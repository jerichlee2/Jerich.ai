Problem.
Let $a$ be a real number and suppose that for all real numbers $x$, it is true that $a\leq{} x^{2}-2x$. Show that $a\leq{} -1$.
Proof. $$\begin{align} a&\leq 1^{2}-2\cdot 1 \\[10pt] &= -1 \end{align}$$
